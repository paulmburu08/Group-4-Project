ABOUT US.

Fund me is a website that was developed to provide a link between people living with disabilities and organizations/well-wishers willing to donate funds to assist them. Organizations and well-wishers can donate through the website and the donations will be updated accordingly. The disabled can then apply for funds that they may require. They can then use those funds to cater for their personal needs, such as food,essential necessities,medical care for them as well as their families.
